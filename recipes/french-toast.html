Brioche French Toast

Description

Thick cut sliced Brioche dipped in cinnamon egg mixture fried in skillet and served with syrup.

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 4 tablespoons butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 8 slices brioche bread
  • 1/2 cup maple syrup

Steps

  1. Combine cinnamon, nutmeg and sugar in a small bowl
  2. Whisk together cinnamon mixture, milk, eggs and vanilla. Pour into shallow container
  3. In a skillet, melt butter on medium heat. Dip bread in egg mixture, fry each side until golden brown
  4. Serve with syrup
